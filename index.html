Project 1: Working Title MovieX

Group Project No 1 - for this project, our team wanted to solve one problem. How do I get dinner and a movie in the meme age? To do that you need to know two things: Where is the movie I want available to stream, and how can I get food that goes with it? We combined the ability to search for movies with their accompanying streaming platform and order food into one app.
